Destinations

Japan

My first ever foreign country. I traveled to Tokyo as an exchange student in 2022 when I was 17. I stayed with a host family and attended high school there for four months.

Recommended tourist spots in Tokyo

Israel

I traveled to Israel for free through the Hillel Birthright program. I visited the Old and New City of Jerusalem, Tel Aviv, Tiberius, Caesarea, Jaffa, Mt. Masada, and a small town next to Gaza.

Jerusalem Tel Aviv Tiberius Caesarea

Canada

Canada is right next door to me in Upstate New York, but surprisingly I've only been there a few times. It's basically just like home, but colder! Niagara Falls was my favorite destination. I've also been to Montreal.

Visit Niagara Falls Explore Montreal

New Zealand

I've spent just over a month in New Zealand, mostly on a beautiful subtropical island off Auckland called Waiheke. I traveled around the Northern mainland, through the countryside of Rotorua, and to various small towns.

Explore Waiheke Island Discover Rotorua

Australia

II've never been so far away from home! From New Zealand, I traveled to Queensland in Northern Australia. Brisbane was a major city I explored. Queensland had incredible natural attractions, including rainforests, beaches, islands, and places to observe Australia's unique wildlife. The wildlife reserves were great for seeing animals up close.

Visit Brisbane Queensland Wildlife Reserves Queensland Beaches Queensland Islands
