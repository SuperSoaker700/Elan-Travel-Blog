Elan's 10 Travel Commandments

Plan Your First Night

Have every detail of your first night planned. After a long travel day, it's best to have a smooth arrival.

More Travel Tips

Document Security

Photocopy your driver’s license, passport, and other important documents. Store digital copies in the cloud.

Required Documents

Purchase Essantials

Buy adapters, SIM cards, and foreign currency at home. This saves money and avoids the hassle of buying on arrival.

More About Travel Essentials

Take Credit Card Precautions

Notify your credit card company of your travel plans. Inquire about foreign transaction fees and charges.

More About Credit Card Safety

Set Up Mobile Service

Contact your carrier about international service. Consider a local SIM card for better rates.

Purchase a SIM Card

Ensure Wi-Fi and Connectivity

Grab a travel Wi-Fi hotspot or add an international data plan. Stay connected without surprise charges.

More About Wi-Fi and Connectivity

Keep a Travel Journal

Keep a journal to record your memories, thoughts, and experiences. It’s a great way to relive your journey.

Purchase a Travel Journal

Pack Smart

Pack versatile, dark-colored clothing for day and night. Keep essentials in your carry-on in case of lost luggage.

Things to Pack

Language Preparation

Carry a phrasebook or translation app. Even if you speak the language, it can be useful in complex situations.

Duolingo

Keep Emergency Contacts

Save local emergency numbers on your phone and know where the nearest embassy or consulate is located.
